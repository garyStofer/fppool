Having a large party and no place to take it ?

Members can reserve the grounds for large outdoor parties, such as Birthdays, Graduation parties and the like. 
Bring some meat and coals and host a pool party for your friends and family.  Here is a list of amenities for you to use: 

  • BBQ pits
  • Picnic Tables
  • Covered Patios
  • Basketball
  • Tetherball
  • Volley Ball Net

To get started with a party, first read here to get all the details on how to party.

Then contact our party host Lanette Stanziano at: E-mail, (925) 408-8017 to make a reservation.

Non members are asked to sign a waiver form when attending events at the pool. Please download the form and fill out for each of your guest in advance. You can turn in the forms with your final paperwork to the party host.
