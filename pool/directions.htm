How to find the cool ...

1766 Mendocino Dr. Concord, CA, 94521, (925) 686 1333

We are conveniently located near the intersection of Concord Blvd. and Mendocino Dr., right across from the Westwood / El Dorado soccer fields. Within a stones throw from the Concord Blvd Neighborhood Park where the 4th of July celebration takes place, this is a great spot to have a BBQ and watch the fireworks from the lawn.

Parking is never a problem, and should our lot ever fill up, the large High School parking is right around the corner. For driving directions click the map below.
