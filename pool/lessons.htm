Public swim lessons

Public swim lesson info for 2021 coming soon! email us at forestparkswimlessons@gmail.com or call Sarah at 925-202-8584

We offer swim lessons to the public for children ages 4 and up.  Classes are for 30 minutes and will not exceed 6 swimmers per instructor.  To sign up for a session please fill out the form here  and bring to the pool on the first day of lessons. All students need to be registered by Thursday the week before the session begins.

If you have any questions please call Sarah Mead at 925-202-8584 or E-mail.
