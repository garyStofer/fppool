Public Swim Lessons

We offer swim lessons to the public for children ages 4 & up.  Classes are for 30 minutes and will not exceed 6 swimmers per instructor.  All swimmers must be registered by the Thursday before the session begins (or call Sarah to check availability 202-8584). Please pick up swim lesson forms at the pool (on the door).  If you have any questions please call Sarah 202-8584 or email PUDGEO@aol.com.

To sign up for a session please print and fill out the form here then drop it off at the pool with your check. All students need to be registered by Thursday the week before the session begins.

Private lessons also available.  Please contact Sarah.
