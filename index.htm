Welcome to the pool at Forest Park !

Pre season swim clinics are starting up soon!

To be competitive in any level of swimming requires more than just a few months of swimming a year. Swimmers need as much time in the water as possible. To find out more and sign your swimmer up click here!

Our swim club is conveniently located across from Concord High and Concord Blvd Neighborhood park. It features two heated pools for your swimming and splashing pleasures. The main pool has 6 competition lanes and a dive board at the deep end. The other side has a shallow area if you like to relax and have fun with the kids. The baby pool is where all the action is for our future master swimmers.

If you have not visited the pool lately come and see the magnificent mural Lyn Bergen painted on the shower wall. It is truly a work of art!

Come for some laps or relax in the shallows after a hard day of work. The water is always nice and the company as pleasant as can be!
