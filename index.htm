Welcome to the pool at Forest Park !

Thank you for your patience.

Things will be a little bit different this summer. We will have a Signup Genius with 2 hour time slots allowing a total of no more than 20 people in the pool. We ask that each family only take one 2 hr. slot per day, however if you are at the pool and the following slot is available when your time is up you may sign up for that one too. Only sign up people that actually enter the pool.

There will be no Lifeguard On Duty and all children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult who monitors the pool.

All members must wear a mask upon entering the facilities and maintain their distance once inside. Mask policy will be monitored and enforced. Masks may be removed while social distancing at 6 feet on the lawn or in the pool. If you need to use the restroom put on your mask. Both front and back Lanai's are closed and all chairs and lounges are currently unavailable.

Entry to the facility will be through the front door and exit through the rear gate only. There will be a 15 minute break in between each 2hr session to allow for cleaning of bathrooms and for the changeover to take place.

Signup Genius for slots  

Our swim club is conveniently located across from Concord High and Concord Blvd Neighborhood park. It features two heated pools for your swimming and splashing pleasures. The main pool has 6 competition lanes and a dive board at the deep end. The other side has a shallow area if you like to relax and have fun with the kids. The baby pool is where all the action is for our future master swimmers.

Come for some laps or relax in the shallows after a hard day of work. The water is always nice and the company as pleasant as can be!

We offer parties to non-members- email fppoolparty@gmail.com to learn more ! Forest Park is an awesome place to have your gathering or birthday party!
